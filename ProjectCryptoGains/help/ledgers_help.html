REFID Reference ID.
Starts with "Q" for deposits.
Starts with "A" or "B" for withdrawals.
Starts with "B" for transfers to Futures wallet or when staking an asset.
Starts with "T" for trades.
Starts with "R" for transfers from Futures wallet, or un-staking an asset, or receiving rewards from staking.
Starts with "S" for staking rewards.
Starts with "MANUAL" for all manual transactions.
DATE Includes both date & time (in UTC time zone).
TYPE "TRADE" = non-margin exchange of one currency for another.
"EARN" = relates to all on-chain staking and Opt-In Rewards activities, including allocation, deallocation, rewards, and other transactions. This unified service encompasses both types of staking under a single category.
"DEPOSIT" = deposit of funds to your Kraken account (including KFEE credits), including transfers from your Kraken Futures wallet or from a credit/debit card purchase (card purchases show a 'deposit' for the full amount charged to the card, then a 'spend' for the amount spent on the asset after the transaction fee is deducted, and 'receive' for the credit of the asset bought).
"WITHDRAWAL" = withdrawal of funds outside of your Kraken account, including transfers to your Kraken Futures wallet.
"TRANSFER" = credit of supported airdrops and forks (e.g. BCH and XLM), and funds transferred to/from Kraken's OTC desk; does not include transfers to/from your Kraken Futures wallet.
"STAKING" = updates to staked balances, including initial staked balances and changes due to the crediting of staking rewards.
EXCHANGE Exchange involved.
CURRENCY Asset Class.
AMOUNT Amount debited (-) or credited (+) to that asset's balance.
FEE Fee paid to Kraken (if any) in the asset.
BALANCE New asset balance after debiting/crediting transaction amount and debiting fee.

balance = old_balance +/- amount - fee
Will be "N/A" for MANUAL transactions.
SOURCE Origin of transaction.
TARGET Goal of transaction.
NOTES Short transaction description.
