Metrics Help

Metrics are generated using the data generated in Process => Ledgers and Process => Trades
  • Total invested represents the sum of all fiat currency deposits recorded in the ledgers
  • Last invested refers to the most recent date on which a fiat currency deposit was recorded in the ledgers
  • Total rewards converted represents the sum of the AMOUNT_FIAT column in the Total Rewards table

Average buy price

CURRENCY Asset
AMOUNT_FIAT Average fiat buy price of asset

Total rewards

CURRENCY Asset
AMOUNT Total rewards amount
AMOUNT_FIAT Total rewards amount converted to fiat (at the opening price of current date UTC)
