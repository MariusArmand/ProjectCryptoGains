REFID Reference ID.
Starts with "T" for trades.
Starts with "MANUAL" for all manual trades.
DATE Includes both date & time (in UTC time zone).
TYPE Can be "BUY" or "SELL"
EXCHANGE Exchange involved.
BASE_CURRENCY Base asset Class.
BASE_AMOUNT Amount debited or credited to the base asset's balance.
BASE_FEE Fee paid to Kraken (if any) in the base asset.
BASE_FEE_FIAT BASE_FEE converted to FIAT.
QUOTE_CURRENCY Quote asset Class.
QUOTE_AMOUNT Amount debited or credited to the quote asset's balance.
QUOTE_AMOUNT_FIAT QUOTE_AMOUNT converted to FIAT.
QUOTE_FEE Fee paid to Kraken (if any) in the quote asset.
QUOTE_FEE_FIAT QUOTE_FEE converted to FIAT.
BASE_UNIT_PRICE The cost of one unit of the base asset in terms of the quote asset.
Calculation: QUOTE_AMOUNT / BASE_AMOUNT
BASE_UNIT_PRICE_FIAT BASE_UNIT_PRICE converted to FIAT.
QUOTE_UNIT_PRICE The cost of one unit of the quote asset in terms of the base asset.
Calculation: BASE_AMOUNT / QUOTE_AMOUNT
QUOTE_UNIT_PRICE_FIAT QUOTE_UNIT_PRICE converted to FIAT.
COSTS_PROCEEDS The costs (in case of "BUY") or proceeds (in case of "SELL").
Calculation:
-COSTS: ABS(QUOTE_AMOUNT) + BASE_FEE_FIAT + QUOTE_FEE_FIAT
-PROCEEDS: ABS(QUOTE_AMOUNT) - BASE_FEE_FIAT - QUOTE_FEE_FIAT
